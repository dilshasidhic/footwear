Fashion footwear is dedicated to designing stylish footwear and accessories that are fashionable and comfortable for customers. Crafted to perfection,Fashion footwear never fail to amaze.This is a Wholesale and retail shop ,we had our own brand handmade footwears

Men's

There are many collections of men’s casual shoes as well as formal, ethnic, sports and party wear styles. These feature moccasins, loafers, sandals and floaters, slippers and flip flops, sneakers, boots and Kolhapuri chappals available in a range of sizes.

Women's

The assortment of women’s footwear features different styles of formal, casual, party, wedding, ethnic and sports shoes. In addition, this massive selection offers sandals, ballerinas, pumps, loafers, peep toes, sneakers, boots, and ethnic shoes with different types of heels.

Kid's

The kids’ collection features formal, casual and school shoes. For boys, we offer sandals, moccasins, loafers, derby and sneakers. Our shoes for girls include sandals, slippers, sneakers and ballerinas.
